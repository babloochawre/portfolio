The Project

Revitalizing an Iconic Brand

Haldiram's is one of India's most beloved food brands, known for its traditional snacks and sweets. This redesign project aimed to modernize the brand while maintaining its rich heritage and cultural significance.

The challenge was to create a visual identity that resonates with younger audiences while staying true to the brand's core values and appealing to its existing customer base.

The new design system incorporates traditional Indian elements with contemporary design principles, creating a perfect balance between heritage and modernity.

Inspiration

"Inspired by Indian tradition, crafted for global appeal"

The design draws inspiration from lotus, diya flame, mandala geometry, festive colors, Indian motifs.The goal was to create a visual language that speaks of authenticity while feeling contemporary and sophisticated.

Key elements were borrowed from traditional Indian art forms, architecture, and textile patterns, reinterpreted through a modern design lens to create a unique identity that stands out in both domestic and international markets.

Design Process

Research & Discovery

Thorough analysis of the brand's history, market position, and competitor landscape to identify opportunities for differentiation.

Concept Development

Creating multiple design directions exploring different aspects of Indian culture and modern design trends.

Visual Identity

Developing the color palette, typography, and graphic elements that form the core of the new brand identity.

Shape Construction

Based on Lotus and Serif typography, creating a distinctive logo mark that represents purity and tradition.

Color System

Selecting colors that represent heritage, celebration, and premium quality while ensuring visual harmony.

Finalization

Refining the chosen direction and preparing the complete brand guidelines for consistent application.

Full Composite Design

A reimagined brand identity for Haldiram’s, inspired by Indian cultural symbols like the lotus and diya. The modern serif logotype, heritage color palette, and versatile applications unify tradition with a global premium appeal.
